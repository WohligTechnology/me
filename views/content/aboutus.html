    About Us

    At flexiME, we are purely facilitating a service by creating a platform that effectively connects flexi-work seekers to project assigners.

    Flexi Talent Sourcing

    flexiME lends to Talent Sourcing, a reliable intermediary role that creates opportunities for people seeking the convenience of flexible working hours, from a place of their choosing. We draw from conventional sourcing mechanisms to create a service model that accurately identifies, orients and refers high calibre talent looking for either part time-flexi time and project based work arrangements from office or home.

    The Flexi-Talent Sourcing (FTS) database comprises career women on maternity or other home related leave, retired senior citizens or even young talent. People with the requisite expertise and experience to handle critical high-value assignments that conventionally engages an in-house skilled team, resulting in high fixed cost and low operational efficiency for corporates.

    flexiME through FTS serves a two-pronged strategy.

    For corporates, our work enhances efficiency and productivity keeping overheads in check

    For qualified professionals assignments that complement their credentials and potential along with the flexibility of working hours.

    In short, our dedicated flexiME website offers both :

    • Talented assets, a much broader, secure and flexible range of project assignments
    • Corporates, a larger and diversified pool of qualified expertise.
